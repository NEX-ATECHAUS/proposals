        <section id="overview" class="section">
            <h2 class="section-title">1. Overview</h2>
            <p>
                This proposal covers the design and delivery of a centralised online portal that connects providers and resalers,
                with the ability to generate custom manufacturer reports for upload into an external portal.
            </p>
            <p>
                The solution uses Nex-a’s low-code stack (<strong>Softr, Xano, and Make</strong>) to balance development speed, cost efficiency,
                and long term scalability. The system is designed to launch quickly with a solid base build, and then layer
                automations on top as usage grows.
            </p>
        </section>

        <!-- Objectives -->
        <section id="objectives" class="section">
            <h2 class="section-title">2. Objectives</h2>
            <div class="grid-two">
                <div>
                    <span class="pill">Business outcomes</span>
                    <ul>
                        <li>Streamline how resalers submit, track, and repeat orders.</li>
                        <li>Give the provider a single source of truth for all resaler activity.</li>
                        <li>Replace manual Excel workflows with structured, searchable data.</li>
                        <li>Reduce time spent compiling updates for the manufacturer.</li>
                    </ul>
                </div>
                <div>
                    <span class="pill">Technical outcomes</span>
                    <ul>
                        <li>Provide secure role-based access for providers and resalers.</li>
                        <li>Implement a clean data model for clients, orders, and statuses.</li>
                        <li>Generate manufacturer-ready CSV or XLSX reports on demand.</li>
                        <li>Create a foundation that can later integrate with CRM, accounting, or AI automations.</li>
                    </ul>
                </div>
            </div>
        </section>
